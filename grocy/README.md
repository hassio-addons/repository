## About

[Grocy][grocy] - ERP beyond your fridge is a powerful groceries & household
management solution for your home, delivering features like:

- Stock management
- Shopping list
- Recipes
- Chores & tasks
- Inventory
- and many more.

[Try out the online demo of Grocy][grocy-demo].
